How to delete your Google Play Games account

To delete your Google Play Games account in the game, you can follow these steps:

  1. Open the Google Play Games app.
  2. Tap on the Profile tab.
  3. Tap on Delete account.
  4. Follow the instructions on the screen to delete your account.

Once you have deleted your account, you will no longer be able to access your Google Play Games data. This includes your achievements, leaderboards, and friends.

If you have any questions about deleting your Google Play Games account, you can contact Google Play support.
